The Business Plan

November 6, 2013

Some people contact us from time to time asking where is our business plan, if we could share it with them. We’d love to, but the truth is that there is no formal business plan. Not any … at all. I know what you are thinking. And to answer your question, there are three reasons why we didn’t set one.

One, because 80 days do not give a lot of time for planning. So we keep improvising.
Two, because the range of possibilities is so big that we can obtain whatever output we want. Anything between bankrupt and Fortune 500.
And three, the most important, because we are driven by passion.

I’m not saying that you should follow our steps. What I am saying is that in our very particular case it won’t help us. We love machines and calculators, but we are not machines nor calculators. Trust me, we can spend our time in more productive tasks right now. But doing this is not motivated by a financial result. We just feel that we have to do it, because we have faith, because we believe in it, because we think it is correct, because we love what we are doing. This is it. There is not any other reason.

So what? Do you like our business plan?
